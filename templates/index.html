Merchant Services (Market)

Our Marketplace offers a secure platform for gamers to facilitate transactions, ensuring peace of mind through verified transactions and escrow services.

Our Marketplace serves as a dynamic platform facilitating transactions between Sellers and Buyers within the gaming community. Sellers utilize this platform to showcase their intention to sell gaming accounts.

Sellers initiate the process by indicating their desire to sell, providing essential details about the gaming account they intend to transfer ownership of.

Once a Seller's listing catches the attention of a potential Buyer, communication ensues, allowing for negotiation on terms, including pricing.

To ensure a secure transaction, Buyers are required to fund their Escrow Account beforehand. This safeguards both parties' interests and mitigates risks associated with fraudulent activities.

Upon mutual agreement between the Seller and Buyer, the Seller proceeds to transfer account credentials securely to the Buyer's designated email address.

With the assurance of funds held in Escrow, Sellers can confidently proceed with the transaction, knowing that payment is guaranteed upon completion.

So in the Market Place we are essentially providing some degree of trust and security to both the seller and buyer and also for Sellers and Buyers to know who to sell their accounts to, or purchase accounts from.

  • - Account credentials are never possessed by any individual or developers throughout the transaction process within our marketplace.
  • - Verification of ownership is automated through IM30 login details provided by sellers, eliminating manual intervention by our developers.
  • - Sellers are not required to provide email login details associated with the game account until the buyer accepts the terms of sale.
  • - Email login details are shared by the seller with the buyer only after the buyer has agreed to proceed with the transaction, ensuring privacy and security.
