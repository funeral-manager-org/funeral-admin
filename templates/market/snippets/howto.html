How to List Game Accounts in Our Marketplace

  1. - Ensure your game account is added to your account list in your Profile dashboard.
  2. - Navigate to Dashboard under Market, then click on >> Start Account Listing.
  3. - A modal box will appear allowing you to fill in your account details, including your game ID, selling price, login email (im30), password, and PIN if available.
  4. - Once you've completed the form, your account will start appearing in your dashboard with a status indicator indicating that the account is not yet listed.
  5. - Click on your account and then add extra details, such as the number of gold sets, gold cards, etc.
  6. - Update your account, and then it will appear publicly.
